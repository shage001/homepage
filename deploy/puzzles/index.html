About

This crossword solver—nicknamed "Regis Fillbin" after the influential "Dr. Fill"—is my senior computer science thesis. It uses a constraint satisfaction approach to reconcile lists of candidate answers for each clue. Answers are gathered from crossword databases, and evaluated based on criteria like how often they appear in other puzzles and how closely they match similar clues. Where necessary, crossing answers are used to find likely solutions.

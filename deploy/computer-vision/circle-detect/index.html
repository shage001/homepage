Circle Detection

By John and Sam

All images use Gaussian smoothing with sigma=2 and Canny edge detection with thresholds of 5 and 10. To improve on our original method, we searched over a range of radii and smoothed the accumulator images (although discretization still proved to be a problem for us because of the ranged search; i.e. smoothing doesn't make a difference when you have ten different accumulator arrays). Our ranged search begins at a minimum radius of 10 and increases by 5-15 (image-dependent) up to the supplied radius.
